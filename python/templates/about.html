About

Here you can use Deep Learning (DNN) to compose one image in the style of another image. You can have your portrait paint like Picasso or Van Gogh... How cool is that? Go ahead and try this app. Just select your own photo and a photo to mimic the style, such as a famous painting, and Wuala!

For more info please visit: tensorflow.org/
